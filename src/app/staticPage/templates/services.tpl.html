Wedding Flowers

Bachman’s wedding department dates back to 1920 when Albert Bachman started transioning the company from growing vegetables to flowers. Albert's wife Olga did all of the original wedding designs without any formal training, she created beautiful flowers by referring to magzine pictures of inspirational.

It's that sort of creativity and ingenuity that continues today among our wedding designers. From traditional favorites to hip new trends, our award-winning team works with hundreds of brides and grooms each year to create the flowers of their dreams.
